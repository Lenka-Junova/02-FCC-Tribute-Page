Moss is great!

Mosses are small, flowerless plants that typically form dense green mats.They are usually to be found in damp or shady places. The plants are usually composed of simple leaves attached to a stem that may be branched or unbranched. They can grow up to 50 cm (20 inches) in height. There are approximately 12 000 species. Mosses are usually able to absorb liquids up to 20 times their own weight. Mosses can be found in peat, they are also used as insulation, they are also used as decoration (gardens, murals, pictures, terrarium).
